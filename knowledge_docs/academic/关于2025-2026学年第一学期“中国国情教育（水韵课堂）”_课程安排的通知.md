# 关于2025-2026学年第一学期“中国国情教育（水韵课堂）” 课程安排的通知

> 来源: https://gs.hhu.edu.cn/2025/0924/c17282a307772/page.htm

各位留学研究生：

“中国国情教育（水韵课堂）”（硕士课程号为2022LM110002，博士课程号为2022LD110002）是留学生的必修课，主要内容为介绍中国与水相关知识。本学期该课程设10个专题讲座 （ 详见附件 1 ） ，现已在 “河海大学 研究生院网络教学 平台 ”上线，每个专题讲座含有讲授视频和考试。学习课程的流程如下：

1. 9 月 24 日前， 在研究生系统完成课程选课，然后 登录 “河海大学 研究生院网络教学 平台 ”注册课程（ 注册 流程 详见附件 2 ） 。

2. 9 月 26 日至 11 月 7 日期间， 在 “河海大学 研究生院网络教学 平台 ”自行安排时间完成线上视频学习及对应的考试（ 具体操作 流程详见附件 2）。

3. 11 月 12 日之后， “河海大学 研究生院网络教学 平台 ” 显示 成绩。成绩构成如下：总成绩 = 完整看完所有课程视频（ 4 0% ） +完成1 0 个专题讲座的考试（ 6 0% ）。成绩大于等于 6 0 分计为通过。

4. 教学平台操作中，如有疑问，可加入QQ群616200173进行咨询。

研究生院

2025年 9 月 22 日

Notice on the Course of Water Harmony Lectures

Dear international graduate students :

The Course of Water Harmony Lectures (2022LM110002 for master and 2022LD110002 for PhD), is a compulsory course required in the program for all international students. The main content is to introduce China's water and related situations. In this semester, the course has 10 special lectures (see Attachment 1 for the detail) , all of which have been launched on the Graduate School Network Teaching Platform of Hohai University . Each special lecture contains teaching videos and ex am . The procedure to take this course is as follows:

1. Before September 24 , please select The Course of Water Harmony Lectures in the graduate system (Those who have already chosen the course can ignore it), and then log in Graduate School Network Teaching Platform of Hohai University to register for courses (see Attachment 2 for the registration process ) .

2. From September 2 6 to November 7 , arrange time to complete online learning and exams in the Graduate School Network Teaching Platform of Hohai University (see Attachment 2 for the specific operation procedure) .

3. After November 12 , Graduate School Network Teaching Platform of Hohai University will view the results.

The grade composition is as follows : Total grade = Watching all course videos in full (40%) + Completing the exams for 10 special lectures (60%). A grade of 60 or above is considered passing.

4. If you have any questions regarding the operation of the teaching platform, you can join the QQ group 616200173 for consultation.
