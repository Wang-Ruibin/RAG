**Notice on the Course of Water Harmony Lectures**

Dear undergraduate students in the “2+2” Dual Degree Program in Port and Waterway Engineering：

The Course of Water Harmony Lectures (F2101203), is a compulsory course required in the program for all international students of “2+2” Dual Degree Program in Port and Waterway Engineering. The main content is to introduce China's water and related situations. In this semester, the course has 10 special lectures, all of which have been launched on the Online Teaching Platform of Hohai University. Each special lecture contains teaching videos and exam. The procedure to take this course is as follows:
